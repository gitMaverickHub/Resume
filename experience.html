PET - Capstone

The Planetary Exploration Transport Vehicle or PET is a vehicle sponsored by NASA that is designed to support future Mars' missions along with any other planetary or lunar mission. The PET should be able to tranport people and cargo through surface translation, global hops, and entering and exiting orbit. Due to the sponshorship with NASA, I am unable to show very much of the PET; however, I am able to show the parts I contributed to. I was on the aerodynamics and propulsion sub-team (A & P) and primarily worked on the Reaction Control System (RCS).

SpaceCRAFT - Research

SpaceCRAFT is a virtual reality system made to be a virtual sandbox for a wide range of aerospace projects from traversal through space to aerospace maneuvers on various planets and moons and more. I did research for SpaceCRAFT. I was on the aerodynamics and propulsion sub-team (A & P) and began to implement the 2D Panel Method into the physics of the virtual reality.

Chemical Rocket Engine Design

This project was to design a chemical rocket engine. We were given an unrealistic goal of performance and efficiency that no rocket engine has achieved in the past. The real goal was to get as close as possible while showing our professor our process of designing the engine. This was a 4 person group project. My main contribution was to the turbopump and pre-burner.
